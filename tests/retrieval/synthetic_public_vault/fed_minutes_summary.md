# Federal Reserve FOMC Minutes — Public Summary

The Federal Open Market Committee publishes meeting minutes three weeks
after each scheduled meeting. The minutes summarize the policy discussion
and dissents.

## Policy Tools

The Committee adjusts the target range for the federal funds rate. Open
market operations and the standing repo facility manage reserves and
short-term funding conditions.

## Communications

The Chair holds a press conference after each meeting in March, June,
September, and December. The Summary of Economic Projections accompanies
the statement on those quarters.
