# Sarbanes-Oxley Compliance Introduction

The Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 imposes financial reporting and internal
control requirements on US public companies. Sections 302 and 404 are the
most frequently cited compliance provisions.

## Section 302 Certifications

The chief executive officer and chief financial officer must personally
certify each periodic report and acknowledge responsibility for the
effectiveness of disclosure controls and procedures.

## Section 404 Internal Control Report

Management must assess and report on the effectiveness of internal control
over financial reporting. Accelerated and large accelerated filers must
also obtain an attestation report from the independent registered public
accounting firm.
