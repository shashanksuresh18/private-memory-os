# Exchange Listing Standards

US national securities exchanges maintain quantitative and qualitative
listing standards that issuers must meet to list and maintain a listing.
Standards differ between primary listing venues.

## Quantitative Tests

Tests include public float, share price, aggregate market value, and
stockholders equity. Exchanges also require minimum holder counts and
recent operating history. Continued listing standards relax certain
thresholds but enforce minimum bid price levels.

## Qualitative Standards

Qualitative requirements include independent director majorities,
independent audit committees, codes of conduct, and shareholder approval
for select equity compensation plans.
