# US Equity Market Structure Primer

US equity markets operate across multiple exchanges and alternative trading
systems. The National Market System ties the venues into a unified quote
and trade tape.

## Order Types

Common order types include market, limit, stop, and stop-limit. Routing
decisions depend on price, size, and the protected quote rule under
Regulation NMS Rule 611.

## Settlement

The US equity settlement cycle moved to T+1 on May 28, 2024. Cash equities
settle one business day after the trade date. Options follow a T+1 cycle as
well.
