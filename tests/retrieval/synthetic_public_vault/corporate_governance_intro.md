# Corporate Governance Introduction

Corporate governance describes the system through which a company is
directed and controlled. The board of directors represents the interests of
shareholders and oversees management.

## Board Structure

Listed-company boards typically include a majority of independent directors
under national exchange listing standards. Standing committees cover audit,
compensation, and nominating responsibilities.

## Shareholder Rights

Common shareholders vote in director elections and on major corporate
actions including mergers, share authorizations, and certain executive
compensation programs. Annual proxy materials disclose these matters.
