# Tender Offer Mechanics

A tender offer is a public bid to purchase outstanding shares of a target
company at a stated price. Tender offers may be friendly or hostile and
are regulated under the Williams Act amendments to the Securities Exchange
Act.

## Minimum Open Period

A tender offer must remain open for at least 20 business days. Material
changes to the offer terms generally extend the open period by at least
ten additional business days from the date of the change.

## Best Price Rule

The best price rule requires the bidder to pay every shareholder the
highest consideration paid to any other shareholder during the tender
offer. Certain employment compensation arrangements are exempt under
amended rules adopted in the mid-2000s.
