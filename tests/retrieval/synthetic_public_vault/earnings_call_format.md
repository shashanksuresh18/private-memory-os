# Public Company Earnings Call Format

A public company earnings call accompanies the release of quarterly
financial results. Calls are typically open to investors and the press
under Regulation Fair Disclosure.

## Standard Structure

The chief executive officer opens with a strategic overview. The chief
financial officer reviews the quarter against guidance. Management closes
the prepared remarks with forward commentary. The session ends with a
question and answer period for covering analysts.

## Disclosures

Forward-looking statements carry a safe-harbor disclaimer. Non-GAAP
metrics include a reconciliation to the most directly comparable GAAP
figures, generally in the press release or supplemental package.
