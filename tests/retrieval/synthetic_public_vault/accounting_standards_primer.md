# US Generally Accepted Accounting Principles Primer

US public companies prepare financial statements under generally accepted
accounting principles. The Financial Accounting Standards Board issues
accounting standards updates that govern recognition, measurement, and
disclosure.

## Revenue Recognition

ASC 606 establishes a five-step model that identifies performance
obligations and recognizes revenue when control transfers to a customer.
Many software and subscription businesses recognize ratably over the
contract term.

## Leases

ASC 842 requires lessees to record right-of-use assets and lease
liabilities on the balance sheet for operating and finance leases.
Disclosures include weighted-average remaining lease term and weighted-
average discount rate.
