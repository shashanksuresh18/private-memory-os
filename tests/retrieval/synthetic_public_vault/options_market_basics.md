# Listed Equity Options Basics

US listed equity options trade on registered options exchanges. The Options
Clearing Corporation acts as the central counterparty for cleared trades.

## Contract Specifications

Each standard contract represents 100 shares of the underlying equity.
Options have a strike price, an expiration date, and a type — call or put.
American-style options can be exercised any time before expiration.

## Margin Requirements

Naked option writing is subject to margin maintenance under FINRA rules.
Cash-secured puts and covered calls have lower margin requirements because
the position is fully collateralized.
