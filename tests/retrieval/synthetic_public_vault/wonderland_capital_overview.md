# Wonderland Capital — Public Firm Overview

Wonderland Capital is a public-equities investment manager headquartered in
New York. The firm publishes a quarterly letter to investors via its public
website.

## Investment Philosophy

Wonderland focuses on long-only positions in mid-cap industrials. The team
uses a fundamentals-driven approach combined with on-the-ground channel
checks. All published positions are post-trade and lag actual portfolio
state by at least 45 days.

## Regulatory Disclosures

Wonderland Capital is registered with the United States Securities and
Exchange Commission as an investment adviser. The firm's most recent ADV
Part 2A brochure is available on the SEC's IAPD website.
