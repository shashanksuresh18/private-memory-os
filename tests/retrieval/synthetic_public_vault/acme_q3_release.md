# Acme Corp Q3 2026 Public Release

Acme Corp announced today the public release of its Q3 product line. The
launch covers the new payments rail, an updated analytics dashboard, and a
free-tier API for hobbyist developers.

## Product Highlights

The payments rail introduces same-day settlement for merchants in the United
States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. The pricing schedule is published
on the Acme investor relations page.
